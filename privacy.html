privacy

how we handle your data — minimal, transparent, never sold

⸻ info we collect

Bilinova collects minimal data. When you browse, we may store:

  • 🖥️ Anonymous usage stats (pages visited, referrer) – via privacy-friendly analytics.
  • 📧 Email address only if you contact us directly or sign up for very rare updates.
  • 🍪 Essential cookies (no tracking, just session integrity).
🔒 we never collect payment details — all transactions are handled by secure external partners.

⸻ how we use

Your data is used only to:

  • ✔️ improve site experience (aggregated analytics)
  • ✔️ reply to your messages (if you contact us)
  • ✔️ send order updates if you purchase (via third‑party shop)

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. Period.

⸻ cookies & tracking

This website uses only strictly necessary cookies (e.g., to maintain session state). We do not use marketing cookies, fingerprinting, or persistent trackers. The optional analytics tool is configured to anonymize your IP address.

  • 🍪 _session (expires when you close browser)
  • 📊 aggregated analytics (opt‑out via browser settings)

You can disable cookies in your browser, but some site features may not work properly.

⸻ third parties

We may use trusted partners for:

  • 📦 E‑commerce platform (Shopify / etc) – they process payments & shipping.
  • 📈 Simple analytics (Plausible / Fathom) – no personal data stored.

Each partner complies with GDPR and similar regulations. Bilinova selects only privacy‑focused services.

⸻ your rights

If you are in the EU/UK, you have the right to:

  • 🔍 request access to your data
  • ✏️ correct inaccuracies
  • 🧹 request deletion (right to be forgotten)

Email privacy@Bilinova.demo – we respond within 30 days.

⸻ retention & security

Logs are kept for 30 days then anonymized. Contact form inquiries are deleted after 6 months. We implement reasonable technical measures to protect your data, though no transmission over the internet is 100% secure.
