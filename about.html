about Bilinova raw. honest. considered.

We don’t follow seasons — we follow texture, drape, and longevity.

Founded in 2025 between London and a small weaving atelier in northern Portugal. Bilinova exists outside the fast‑fashion cycle. Every piece is made in limited runs, using deadstock or responsibly sourced fabrics.

craft

Each pattern is cut by hand in our Lisbon studio. We work with sewers who have been in the trade for over twenty years — no mass production.

community

Bilinova is shaped by a slow community of wearers and makers. We host fitting events and fabric swaps — physical, not virtual.

design

Unconstructed silhouettes, raw edges, and minimal hardware. Our pieces age — they aren’t meant to stay “perfect”.

slow creation

We travel twice a year to meet our weavers and dyers. No moodboards — we react to what they have in stock, often deadstock fabrics that would otherwise go to landfill. That’s why each collection is small and unrepeatable.

Bilinova is not a brand; it’s a rotating archive of garments that respect the hands that made them.

— Bilinova studio
